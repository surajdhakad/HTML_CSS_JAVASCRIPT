Suzuki Motorcycle India has burst into the scene with the middleweight parallel-twin GSX-8R. Priced at Rs 9.25 lakh, ex-showroom Delhi. The sports bike was earlier in the year showcased at the Bharat Mobility Global Expo 2024. Even though the fully faired GSX-8R shares the same platform as the naked GSX-8S, it is powered by the same 776 cc parallel-twin engine as the V-Strom 800 DE.
